Formula 1

The Premier Global Motorsport

Formula 1 (F1) is the highest class of international racing for open-wheel, single-seater cars. It is sanctioned by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile. The F1 World Championship has been sought after by teams and drivers since 1950.

F1 seasons are yearly with around 20 races a year called Grand Prix. Races are split into practice sessions, qualifying sessions, and the Grand Prix itself

F1 cars are the fasted regulated road-course cars in the world and are immensely high-tech. For years, F1 has pushed the boundaries of automotive science.
